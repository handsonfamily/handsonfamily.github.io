About Us

WoFoo

Background

Initiated and organized by Wofoo Social Enterprises 和富社會企業 (www.wse.hk). The campaign would like to promote care and love of families and the community in Hong Kong.

Introduction

The hands are where the minds meet the world. These assemblies of 27 small bones can make homes, comfort and support our loved ones. As we mark the 20th anniversary of the International Year of Family, this platform is to share and to celebrate how our hands builds families and inter-generation relations in their special ways.

Objective

To feature the hands as a focal point to propagate messages (written, visual and audio-visual), and highlight the importance, of support and love for the family through the new media as part of the campaign.

Title/ Theme
